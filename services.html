01.

What does MOSO do, exactly?

MOSO is a design and technology studio that helps businesses build their brand, digital presence, and growth systems. Services include brand strategy and identity, website and digital product design and development, SEO, AEO, content marketing, paid advertising, and workflow automation. Think of us as a general contractor for brand, digital, and growth — one thoughtful partner instead of ten disconnected vendors.

02.

What is AEO and why does it matter?

AEO stands for Answer Engine Optimization — structuring your website and content so AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews can accurately find, understand, and cite your business. As more people turn to AI for answers before ever clicking a search result, AEO has become as important as traditional SEO. MOSO builds every site with AEO in mind: structured data, FAQ schema, entity clarity, semantic content architecture, and clear brand signals.

03.

What is the difference between SEO and AEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps your site rank in traditional search results. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) helps your content appear in AI-generated answers from tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. SEO focuses on keywords, backlinks, and technical health. AEO focuses on entity recognition, direct answers, structured data, topical authority, and content that AI can read and cite. MOSO optimizes for both — ensuring clients are visible wherever their audience is searching, now and in the future.

04.

What is the MOSO method?

The MOSO method moves through five phases. Clarify: we identify what the business is, who it serves, and what needs to change. Design: we shape the message, identity, experience, and direction. Build: we develop the site, system, product, or infrastructure needed. Launch: we put the work into motion cleanly and strategically. Refine: we improve based on performance, feedback, and future goals. Every engagement follows this arc, whether we are building a logo or an entire brand ecosystem.

05.

What makes MOSO different from other agencies?

Most agencies are strong in one lane — visuals, or copy, or technical builds — but weak in the others. MOSO bridges creative and technical work under one roof. We also think beyond the immediate deliverable. Every project considers scalability, systems, automation, AI readiness, and long-term brand positioning. The goal is never just to make something look better. It is to make it work better, feel more aligned, and be easier to grow.

06.

Does MOSO build Webflow websites?

Yes. MOSO builds websites using Webflow, Framer, Next.js, and custom React depending on the scope, complexity, and long-term needs of each project. Webflow is used for marketing sites that need speed, maintainability, and a strong CMS. Next.js and React are used for more complex custom applications, portals, and scalable web products. Every build is optimized for performance, SEO, and AEO from the ground up.

07.

What industries does MOSO work with?

MOSO works across a wide range of industries including hospitality and restaurants, wellness and mental health, lifestyle and luxury, creative and cultural brands, startups and digital products, professional services, biotech and life sciences, finance and fintech, and mission-driven companies. The common thread across all clients is ambition — a business that wants its brand and digital presence to match the quality of what it actually delivers.

08.

How long does a typical project take?

Every project is scoped individually. A focused brand identity or landing page typically takes two to four weeks. A comprehensive website with custom design and development takes four to eight weeks. A full brand ecosystem — including identity, website, content systems, and growth infrastructure — can take two to four months. Timelines are agreed upon before work begins, and you are kept informed at every stage.

09.

How is pricing structured?

MOSO uses project-based pricing tailored to each engagement. Scope, goals, and budget are discussed upfront, and a transparent proposal is provided before any work begins. There are no opaque retainers or vague hourly billing. For ongoing partnerships, structured monthly arrangements are available once the initial project is complete.

10.

How collaborative is the process?

Highly collaborative. Every engagement begins with a deep discovery conversation to understand your business, your goals, and the real obstacles in your way. From there, we work in structured phases with clear checkpoints, feedback rounds, and decision points. You are never left guessing where things stand. The right level of involvement varies by project — some clients are hands-on at every step; others prefer to review and approve at key milestones.

11.

Does MOSO work with startups?

Yes. MOSO works with startups, growing businesses, and established brands. Early-stage companies often need help building their brand and digital foundation from scratch — positioning, identity, website, and initial growth infrastructure. Established businesses often need MOSO to help them evolve past what they have outgrown. Both are rewarding in different ways.

12.

Can MOSO help with content strategy and SEO?

Yes. MOSO provides content strategy, blog strategy, on-page SEO, local SEO, and AEO as integrated services. Content is not treated as an afterthought — it is built into the architecture of every site we design. This means proper heading hierarchy, semantic structure, schema markup, topic clusters, internal linking, and content that AI answer engines can read, understand, and cite. Good content and good SEO are the same thing when done right.

13.

Does MOSO use AI in its work?

Yes — thoughtfully. MOSO uses AI to accelerate research, content drafting, automation design, and technical workflows. But AI is always guided by human strategy, judgment, and creative direction. The goal is work that feels deeply human and highly capable — not generic AI output. MOSO also helps clients implement AI-assisted tools internally, from content systems to operational workflows.

14.

Does MOSO offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes. Most long-term client relationships go well beyond the initial project. MOSO offers ongoing support, maintenance, growth retainers, and creative partnerships for brands that want a long-term creative and technical partner. The studio is not interested in one-and-done relationships. The goal is to help clients build something that keeps getting better.

15.

Is MOSO's approach to marketing ethical?

Yes. MOSO believes marketing should be intelligent, emotionally aware, and durable — not manipulative, spammy, or low-trust. The studio does not use dark patterns, deceptive copy, false urgency, or tactics that erode the relationship between a brand and its audience. Growth that is built on honesty, clarity, and genuine value is the only kind worth building.

16.

What is MOSO's approach to automation?

MOSO designs automation systems to reduce friction and support scale — not to replace the human thinking at the core of a business. This includes workflow automation, lead routing, CRM integration, AI-assisted internal tools, content pipelines, and connected digital ecosystems. Technology should make life simpler and operations stronger, not bury a business under complexity.

How do I get started with MOSO?

The first step is a conversation. Visit the contact page, send a message describing your project and goals, and someone from MOSO will respond within one business day. From there, a discovery call is scheduled to understand your business, scope the right engagement, and determine whether there is a strong mutual fit. There is no pressure and no obligation.

18.

Is MOSO the right partner for my business?

MOSO is a strong fit for businesses that care about quality, want to stand out with clarity and intention, and are ready to invest in building something that will last. It is especially well-suited for brands that feel their current brand or website does not match the quality of what they actually offer — and who want one thoughtful partner who can help them bring the whole picture together.
